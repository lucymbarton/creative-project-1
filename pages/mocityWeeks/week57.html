Buenos días!

It was another relatively slow week in Missouri City. Although I got news last night that I'm going to be ET'd (Emergency Transferred) back to the Jones Creek/Grand Mission area. But President Guthrie said he's going to buy some English name tags for me and that he feels like I'll be able to help the area a lot. So it'll be a new adventure, even though it will be hard.

I went on exchanges with Sister Lee and Sister Lafleur in Sienna Plantation and we taught a lady they are teaching named Sam and her less active boyfriend. They had a lot of questions about eternal families and the spirit world and it was really fun to answer them. I hope they get married soon so that Sam can get baptized!

We went to church on Sunday and it was great! I love Hispanic people, they are so faithful. And I love being able to understand them. I will miss Spanish church and the members.

I think that's about it for this week.

Hello again everyone! Happy July!

This week was rather uneventful... we called a lot of people who flaked out on us. But for the 4th of July we made our own hamburgers and deviled eggs and keto raspberry cheesecake! It was very yummy! Then we watched fireworks from our porch! It was awesome!

We're going to have to push back Lesly's baptism. We're still waiting to hear how bad her knee injury is.

It was kind stressful since I didn't know that my mission president was literally going to be sitting there listening the entire time... but turns out I need to work on my How to Begin Teaching. My President said I speak Spanish like I've already been here a year!

We had a stake-wide devotional last night and they announced that in-person church will be starting up soon! There are a million rules but still pretty exciting.

Love you all

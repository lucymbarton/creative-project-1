Week 32

Buenos dias!!!

Another week of the mish!

Last P-day was boring since our elders were still in quarentine.

Tuesday we had to help teach/translate 2 English Connect classes! Shout out to my parents for buying us Chinese Food take-out! Yeet! We went out to our trailer park and we taught a man named Blas. He went off for like half an hour trying to say we were wrong and he just kept telling us stories from the bible and I got soooooo frustrated with him. I was like "Si lea este libro con intencion verdadera va a saber por si mismo que es la verdad!!!" And apparently he was offended last time we talked to him because I wouldn't tell him my first name... so yeah it was just a pretty useless interaction since he didn't actually have intentions to listen to us.

Wednesday we had District Council online and Hermana Cannefax made us fried plantains! We went out and taught three lessons in the trailer park with a recently returned missionary from our ward Elder I mean Brother Kelly. I was having a rough time since I got another ear infection :( but the Elders brought us split pea and ham soup and Blue Bell ice cream :) I think they're in love with us lol.

Thursday we drove out to Sugarland to see my ear doctor. He put a little sponge in my ear so that the drops actually go in. Pretty nice but the only thing is that even though my ear feels better now I can't hear because there's still a fat sponge in my ear... My Doctor said sometimes it falls out on its own but I'll go back there next Thursday for a follow up appointment and he'll take it out then. Also on Thursday we had Atonement Training over Zoom for Hermana Oveson's training.

Friday we finally got to have our interviews with President. He asked me about my emotional health. I was like "yeah I'm doing pretty great just wish I didn't have an ear infection lol"

Saturday we went and served at the Parish's house again and they have a lady who lives with them named Rachel who is not a member of our church and we invited her to attend church with the Parish's and she did!!! We finally got someone to come to church!!!!

So update on my goal to read the whole bible: I'm on page 404 of the Old Testament. It's finally starting to get good lol. This week I had fallen behind in my reading schedule since I slept in a few days with my ear infection so I had to catch up one day and I read 18 chapters in 1 hour. Pretty nice.

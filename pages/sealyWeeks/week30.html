Week 30

Buenas tardes!

This week seemed soo long!

On tuesday we went and picked up our new companion Hermana Oveson! She is from Nephi, Utah in the middle of nowhere. She feels at home here in Sealt. She was supposed to come out when I came out but she was sent home in March from the MTC because she had diabetes. She is so cute and so ready to learn and be a missionary!!

We had my first exchanges this week. I had to wake up at 5:45 two days in a row and drive all the way to Richmond to switch companions. Hermana Oveson and I spent the day with Sister Lewis. We found a lady named Vanessa and I talked to her even without Hermana Cannefax.

We went to the Bay's house and they gave us jalepeno poppers with duck wrapped in bacon. And they made kabobs with ram. It was good.

Conference was awesome!!!!
